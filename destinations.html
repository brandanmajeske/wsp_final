Airports

Orlando International

Orlando International is located in the business district of Orlando and is considered the second busiest airport in Florida, behind Miami International Airport. Orlando Int'l is a focus city for Southwest Airlines and JetBlue Airways.

Airports

Orlando Sanford

Orlando Sanford International Airport sits on approximately 2000 acres, located in Seminole County, Florida. It has four runways and serves many regional and international carriers. Aerosim Flight Academy calls the airport home.

Airports

Orlando Executive

Orlando Executive is located in Orange County, Florida on 1055 acres, has two runways and serves many private businesses in the area. The airport is often used as the landing site for Marine One during Presidential visits.

Attractions

Epcot
Disney World

Located near Orlando, Epcot is a theme park in the Walt Disney World Resort. The name Epoct comes from an acronym for a utopian city conceptualized by Walt Disney called the Experimental Prototype Community of Tomorrow. Epcot is the 5th most-visited theme park in the world.

Attractions

Universal Orlando Resort

A popular destination for entertainment, Universal Orlando Resort is a theme park resort located in Orlando, Florida. The resort consists of Universal Studios Florida, Islands of Adventure, and Universal CityWalk along with multiple hotels.

Attractions

Orlando Science Center

The Orlando Science Center is a popular attraction in Central Florida. The Science Center has four floors of exhibits, an observatory, and a domed theater for planetarium shows. Educational and great fun for all ages!

Convention Centers

Orange County Convention Center

The OCCC is known as the primier convention center for Central Florida. It is the second largest convention center in the United States, behind McCormick Place in Chicago, Illinois. The convention center boasts over 2 million square feet of exhibition space!

Convention Centers

The Peabody Hotel

The Peabody Hotel is situated near the Orange County Convention Center. The hotel is 31 stories tall, contains over 1600 rooms and is the second tallest building in Orlando.

Convention Centers

Orlando World Center Marriott

The 28 story Marriott hotel is the largest Marriott in the world. With access to a golf course, a large ballroom, six swimming pools and 10 restaurants and lounges, it is a great place for business meetings or vacationing.

Ports

Port Canaveral

One of the busiest ports in the world, Port Canaveral, located on the eastern coast of Florida, is used by cruise and cargo ships. The port contains six cruise terminals and is the home port for ships from Disney Cruise Lines, Carnival Cruise Lines and Royal Caribbean.

Ports

Port of Tampa

The port of Tampa is the largest port in Florida, located on the western coast of the state. The port is one of the most popular points of embarkation for Caribbean cruises and is home to cruise terminals for Carnival Cruise Line, Royal Caribbean Int'l, Holland America Line and Norwegian Cruise Line.

Ports

Port Manatee

Port Manatee is one of the largest deepwater seaport in Florida and is served by mostly cargo ships. The port is 1100 acres in size and moves about 9 million tons of cargo per year.

Arenas

Amway Center

The Amway Center is located in Downtown Orlando, and home to sports teams like the Orlando Magic and the Orlando Predators. The venue is also used to host concerts and events.

Arenas

Kissimmee Sports Arena

Kissimmee Sports Arena hosts the Monthly Saturday Rodeo, and other exciting events. The Banquet Hall has a 3200 square foot lounge available for special occasions, weddings, family or class reunions and conventions.

Arenas

Silver Spurs Arena

Located in the Osceola Heritage Park entertainment complex, Silver Spurs Arena is an 8000 seat venue and home to the Silver Spurs Rodeo.


Taxi

Taxis can hold up to 4 passengers and are ideal transportation for trips to an airport, hotel, a sports arena or a night club.

Sport Utility

SUVs can hold up to 7 passengers and are great for trips to many of Central Florida's theme parks and attractions.

Sedan

Sedans can hold up to 4 passengers in comfort and style while traveling to places like the airport or convention center.

Limousine

Limousines can hold up to 10 passengers and are ideal for any special occasion, like a wedding, or a night on the town.
