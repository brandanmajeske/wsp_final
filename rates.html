Taxi

Taxis can hold up to 4 passengers and are ideal transportation for trips to an airport, hotel, a sports arena or a night club.

Sport Utility

SUVs can hold up to 7 passengers and are great for trips to many of Central Florida's theme parks and attractions.

Sedan

Sedans can hold up to 4 passengers in comfort and style while traveling to places like the airport or convention center.

Limousine

Limousines can hold up to 10 passengers and are ideal for any special occasion, like a wedding, or a night on the town.
